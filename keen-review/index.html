    Our Full Review

    Keen Overview

    Keen has been connecting seekers with psychic advisors since 1999. The platform is known for its large network of advisors and competitive pricing, making psychic readings accessible to everyone.

    Finding Your Advisor

    Keen’s search and filtering system is among the best in the industry. Filter by specialty, reading type, price range, and customer rating to quickly narrow down your options from thousands of available advisors.

    Mobile Experience

    The Keen mobile app is well-designed and full-featured. You can browse advisors, start readings, and manage your account from your phone with the same functionality as the desktop site.

    Our Verdict

    Keen delivers excellent value with competitive pricing and a massive selection of advisors. The filtering tools make it easy to find quality readers, and the mobile app adds convenience. A solid choice for regular psychic consultation.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Keen’s $1.99/10-min offer the real price?

    Yes — for new accounts only. The promo caps at 10 minutes with any advisor who has opted in. After the promo clock expires, you roll onto the advisor’s normal per-minute rate automatically.

    How does Keen vet its advisors?

    Applicants submit background info, sample readings, and are rated by the first wave of paying callers. Keen tracks per-minute cancellation rate and dispute rate — advisors above thresholds are removed.

    Is my phone number private if I use the call feature?

    Yes. Keen connects both parties through a masked proxy number. The advisor cannot see your real number.

    Can I get a refund?

    Keen does not offer cash refunds but does issue account credit on a case-by-case basis if a reading was cut by a technical fault or you flag an issue within 24 hours.

    Are chat, phone, and video all available?

    Chat and phone are the core. Video is only available with a subset of premium advisors — look for the video icon on their profile.

    Can I tip an advisor after a reading?

    Yes. Keen supports post-session tips and they are non-refundable, so tip only after you’ve reflected on the session quality.
