    The methodology · Version 4.2 · April 2026

    How we rate. And how we don't.

    Every rating is earned through anonymous sessions, structured rubrics, and reviewer panels. Our methodology is public, our reviewers are paid by us, and no platform pays for placement. Ever.

    The six factors

    What goes into a score

    01 WEIGHT 30%

    Accuracy

    Measured against pre-session control facts known only to the reviewer. Scored across nine sub-dimensions.

    02 WEIGHT 20%

    Reader vetting

    We audit each platform's applicant rejection rate, credential verification, and probationary process.

    03 WEIGHT 15%

    Pricing

    Transparency, surprise-fee incidence, billing accuracy. Tested through 600+ checkouts per platform per cycle.

    04 WEIGHT 15%

    Experience

    Session interface, reader-side tooling, post-session support. Includes panel-rated qualitative reviews.

    05 WEIGHT 10%

    Support

    Median response time, dispute resolution outcomes, refund policy clarity. Tested via real incidents.

    06 WEIGHT 10%

    Privacy

    Data retention, anonymity options, third-party sharing. Audited annually by independent counsel.

    The process

    From applicant to rating

    1. 01

      Eligibility audit

      We confirm the platform meets minimum operating standards: business registration, dispute mechanism, age-verification.

    2. 02

      Reviewer panel assembly

      Twelve anonymous reviewers from our 47-person panel are matched to the platform's discipline.

    3. 03

      Session protocol

      Each reviewer runs four to six sessions with different readers, following a structured rubric.

    4. 04

      Aggregation

      Scores are aggregated, outliers reviewed, and the editorial team writes the long-form review.

    5. 05

      Annual re-audit

      Every platform is re-rated annually. Significant changes trigger an off-cycle review.

    Our commitments

    Four lines we don't cross.

    01.

    No sponsored placements

    Not now, not ever. Our revenue is reader subscriptions and affiliate fees that flow only after a rating is published — and are disclosed.

    02.

    No advance access

    Platforms do not see scores before publication. We answer factual questions, not editorial ones.

    03.

    Anonymous reviewers

    Our panel is paid, vetted, and protected. Platforms cannot identify or target our reviewers.

    04.

    Published methodology

    Every change to the rubric is documented and dated. The current version is 4.2, dated April 2026.

    At Trusted-Psychic-Ratings.net, our mission is to provide you with unbiased, reliable, and transparent reviews of psychic services. We understand that finding a genuine psychic can be a deeply personal and often challenging journey. That’s why we’ve developed a rigorous, multi-faceted methodology to ensure that the ratings and reviews you see on our site are truly trustworthy. Our commitment is to empower you with the information you need to make informed decisions.

    Our Independent Tester Network

    The cornerstone of our review process is our network of independent testers. These individuals are not affiliated with any psychic service and are carefully selected for their ability to provide objective feedback. We ensure a diverse range of testers, representing various backgrounds and perspectives, to minimize individual biases and provide a comprehensive evaluation. Each tester undergoes a thorough vetting process to confirm their commitment to our ethical guidelines and their understanding of our evaluation criteria.

    • Anonymous & Unbiased: Our testers engage with psychics anonymously, just as any regular client would. This ensures they receive an authentic experience, free from any special treatment due to their testing role.
    • Detailed Reporting: After each session, testers complete a comprehensive report, detailing their experience, the psychic’s communication style, accuracy of readings, and overall impression.
    • No Prior Affiliations: We strictly prohibit testers from having any past or current professional or personal affiliations with the psychic services they are assigned to review.

    Our Comprehensive Evaluation Criteria

    To ensure consistency and fairness across all reviews, our independent testers evaluate psychics against a standardized set of criteria. These criteria are designed to cover all aspects of a psychic reading, from initial contact to the depth of insight provided. We believe a truly valuable psychic experience encompasses more than just predictive ability; it also includes empathy, clarity, and professionalism.

    • Accuracy of Readings: This is a primary focus. Testers evaluate how well the psychic’s insights resonate with their past, present, and potential future circumstances, without providing leading information.
    • Clarity and Communication: How clearly does the psychic convey their messages? Is their language easy to understand? Do they avoid vague or generalized statements?
    • Empathy and Connection: Does the psychic demonstrate genuine empathy and compassion? Do they create a comfortable and supportive environment for the reading?
    • Professionalism and Ethics: This includes punctuality, respect for privacy, adherence to ethical guidelines, and avoiding making guarantees or giving legal/medical advice.
    • Insight and Depth: Does the psychic offer more than surface-level observations? Do they provide actionable advice or deeper understanding of situations?
    • Value for Money: Considering the cost of the service, does the reading provide a worthwhile experience and valuable insights?

    The Scoring and Rating Process

    Once a tester submits their detailed report, our editorial team meticulously reviews the findings. Each psychic is typically reviewed by multiple independent testers to ensure a robust and multi-perspective evaluation. The scores from these individual reports are then aggregated and weighted according to our established methodology.

    • Individual Score Aggregation: Each tester assigns a score based on the criteria outlined above. These individual scores are then combined.
    • Weighted Averages: Certain criteria, such as “Accuracy of Readings” and “Insight and Depth,” carry a higher weighting in our overall scoring system, reflecting their importance in a quality psychic reading.
    • Qualitative Analysis: Beyond numerical scores, our editorial team also performs a qualitative analysis of the testers’ written feedback. This helps us understand the nuances of the experience and identify common themes or exceptional insights.
    • Overall Rating: A composite score is then generated, which translates into the final star rating you see on our website. We also provide a summary of the key strengths and weaknesses identified by our testers.

    Our Commitment to Transparency and Continuous Improvement

    Transparency is at the heart of everything we do. We believe you deserve to know exactly how we arrive at our ratings. Our methodology is regularly reviewed and updated to ensure it remains fair, comprehensive, and reflective of the evolving psychic services landscape. We are committed to providing you with the most accurate and helpful information possible.

    • Regular Audits: Our methodology and scoring system are periodically audited by an internal review board to ensure consistency and prevent bias.
    • Feedback Integration: We welcome feedback from our users and testers. This feedback is invaluable in helping us refine our processes and improve our service.
    • Ethical Guidelines: All testers and editorial staff adhere to strict ethical guidelines, ensuring integrity and impartiality throughout the review process.
