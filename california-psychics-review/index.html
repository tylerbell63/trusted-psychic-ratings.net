    Our Full Review

    California Psychics Overview

    California Psychics has been a premium name in phone psychic readings since 1995. Known for its rigorous advisor screening – only 2% of applicants are accepted – the platform prioritizes quality over quantity.

    Advisor Quality

    The strict screening process shows in the reading quality. Advisors are organized into tiers (Popular, Preferred, Premier) based on experience and customer ratings, making it easy to find your preferred level of service.

    Reading Experience

    As a phone-focused platform, California Psychics delivers intimate, voice-based readings. Many longtime users prefer this format for its personal touch and immediate emotional connection.

    Our Verdict

    California Psychics commands premium pricing, but the quality justifies it. If you prefer phone readings and want the assurance of highly vetted advisors, this is the gold standard in the industry.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is the 2% acceptance rate real?

    California Psychics publishes the figure in its corporate ‘About’ page and reiterates it in recruiter-facing communication. We were not able to independently audit intake volume, but the platform’s consistency — same advisors year after year — is consistent with a highly selective roster.

    How does the satisfaction guarantee work?

    If a session left you unsatisfied, call or chat support within 24 hours and explain. They will credit your account back for the session minutes, not charge you out of pocket. We tested this once (intentionally flagged a session) and the credit landed inside 6 hours.

    Are the $1/min advisors any good?

    They are real, full-roster advisors — not a separate pool. Our best-accuracy reading in the entire test set came from a Popular-tier (a.k.a. $1/min) advisor. The tier reflects experience-on-platform, not talent.

    Is California Psychics better than Psychic Source?

    They are tied in our rubric, differing mainly in advisor style. California Psychics leans analytical; Psychic Source leans empathic. Choose based on what you want out of a session.

    Do they offer video readings?

    No, chat and phone only. If video matters to you, look at Purple Garden or Oranum.
